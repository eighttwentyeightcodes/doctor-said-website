About Dr. Said

Board‑certified Periodontology & Implantology specialist. Member of the German Association of Dental Implantology (DGZI).

10+ Years Experience

Dr. Said Hussein Ali is a board‑certified Periodontist and Implantologist dedicated to restorative excellence and patient‑centered care. For over a decade, he has focused on comprehensive periodontal therapy and the full spectrum of implant dentistry—from single‑tooth replacement to complex, full‑arch rehabilitation—meticulously planning every case for long‑term function and natural esthetics.

He is an active member of the German Association of Dental Implantology (DGZI), a global community of implant experts committed to rigorous standards, continuing education, and innovation. This international affiliation keeps his clinical approach aligned with the latest evidence‑based protocols, digital workflows, and biomaterials science.

Dr. Ali’s training blends surgical precision with restorative collaboration, ensuring harmonious outcomes across soft tissue, bone, and prosthetics. His philosophy emphasizes clear communication, comfort, and informed decision‑making—so every patient understands their options and feels confident at each step.

Beyond clinical practice, he is passionate about prevention and maintenance, helping patients sustain periodontal health with tailored home‑care strategies and structured follow‑up. Whether addressing complex implant cases or minimally invasive soft‑tissue procedures, his goal is simple: stable, beautiful results that last.
