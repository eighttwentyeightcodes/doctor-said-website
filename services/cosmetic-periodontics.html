What is Cosmetic Periodontics?

Cosmetic periodontal procedures reshape the gumline to improve symmetry, correct a "gummy smile", and expose more natural tooth for improved esthetics and restorative outcomes.

Benefits

  • Balanced, symmetrical smile
  • Improved tooth proportions
  • Better foundation for veneers/crowns
  • Minimally invasive techniques

Treatment Options

1

Gum Contouring

Precise reshaping of the gumline to correct uneven or excessive gums for improved esthetics.

2

Crown Lengthening

Expose more natural tooth by adjusting gum and bone levels to create ideal proportions.

3

Tissue Recontouring

Subtle refinement of soft tissues around teeth or implants for natural contours.

4

Smile Design & Planning

Collaborative planning to harmonize periodontal esthetics with restorative goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will I see results?

Most cosmetic periodontal procedures show immediate improvement, with final tissue settling over a few weeks.

Is there downtime?

Downtime is minimal. We provide tailored aftercare instructions for comfort and rapid healing.

Can this be combined with veneers or crowns?

Yes, cosmetic periodontal procedures often complement restorative treatments for optimal esthetics.
